National Dish Project

Embark on an exciting voyage through the Data analysis workflow with 173 diverse national cuisines.

About The National Dish Project

The project aims to promote a better grasp of the usual data analysis workflow among non-technical audiences by utilizing a tailored dataset that includes 173 classic dishes from different countries.

Project Goals

  1. In just one sitting, ensure that 90% of the project's audience easily digests two-thirds of the workflow.
  2. Enhance the project's audience engagement by 30% by incorporating visual aids in the communication of technical details.
  3. Harness data to fuel debates on the age-old dispute regarding the health benefits of white meat versus red meat.
